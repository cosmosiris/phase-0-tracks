Dealing With My Critical Inner Voice

Throughout my DBC program it will be very important for me to think positively and practice positive self-talk. Below are some tips to help me succeed

Daily Practices

  • Create a schedule of days & times to complete assignments and stick to it
  • Exercise 4-5 days a week
  • Schedule time to practice typing and memory skills
  • Schedule time to learn extra skills not covered in the week's materials
  • Give myself at least 2 hours a day of leisure, preferrably in the evenings before I go to sleep

Steps for Dealing with a Negative Thought that is impairing my ability to focus

  1. Identify the negative thought
  2. Identify the trigger for this negative thought
  3. Determine my options. For example, if my negative thought is telling me to give up and try again tomorrow then I have at least 3 options:
    1. Give up and try again tomorrow
    2. Take a short 5-10 minute break and then continue working
    3. Ignore the negative thought and continue working till my scheduled end time
  4. Then determine the potential outcomes of each option. List the pros and cons of each outcome
  5. Then make a decision that is intelligent and well thought out. In the example above I would most likely realize that procrastinating will set me behind schedule which could result in a lot of other poor outcomes like having to cancel plans that I've made, or not being prepared for a mandatory peer to peer work session.
